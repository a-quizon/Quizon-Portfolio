Our Story

Humble Beginnings

Dina's Mami House started in 2010 as a small food cart in the neighborhood. Driven by Tita Dina's passion for cooking heartwarming Filipino comfort food, the stall quickly became a local favorite. From a single pot of lugaw, we have grown into a cozy dine-in space, offering a variety of congee and noodle soups.

We believe in using only fresh ingredients, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and a lot of love in every bowl.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, delicious, and comforting Filipino meals that bring people together, making every customer feel right at home.

Our Vision

To be the go-to neighborhood mami house recognized for authentic taste, excellent service, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Why Choose Us?

Customers love our food because we don't cut corners. Rich broths, tender meats, and perfectly seasoned toppings make our dishes unforgettable.
